About Nipah

Nipah is a revolutionary memecoin dedicated to the fight against the Nipah virus. Born from the community's desire to make a positive impact, each transaction with Nipah contributes to research and aid for those affected by this deadly disease. Not only has it become a symbol of hope in the crypto world, but Nipah also stands as a beacon of unity and resilience, reminding us of the power of collective action in the face of adversity !
